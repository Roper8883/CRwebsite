Regional logic

Different regions, different terminology, same product philosophy.

The family structure matters because electrician pathways, portfolio language, and qualification contexts differ by region. The brand architecture helps make that separation clear while keeping a recognisable product family.

Shared principles

What stays consistent across the family.

Even with regional separation, the broader Sparky's Ready family keeps a consistent point of view about readiness, support, trust, and trade-aware workflow design.

Trade-aware

Built around real electrical-training and workplace patterns

The products aim to feel like serious trade tools, not generic learning portals.

Workflow-led

Profiling, learning, practice, and progress stay connected

Each product is built around readiness flow, not just one isolated content type.

Trust-first

Support, legal, evidence, and billing are part of the experience

The family website and legal centre are designed to reinforce trust instead of hiding important detail in thin footer pages.
