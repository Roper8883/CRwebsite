Privacy Policy

Version 1.3 | Effective date: 25 February 2026

Spark's Capstone Ready (Australia) provides CapstoneReady. Contact: capstoneready@outlook.com.

Compliance notice: CapstoneReady is a preparation/training support app and is not a Registered Training Organisation (RTO).

1. Scope

This policy explains how personal information is handled in CapstoneReady in an Australian context, aligned with the Australian Privacy Principles.

2. Information we collect

  • Sign-in identifiers and account profile details from selected providers (Apple, Google, Facebook, Email).
  • Learning progress data including attempts, scores, readiness, and module completion.
  • Weekly profiling records, including recipient emails and supervisor sign-off fields.
  • Premium entitlement state from Apple StoreKit.
  • Operational diagnostics required for support and app stability.

3. Why we use information

  • Provide app access and features.
  • Track progress and training preparation outcomes.
  • Deliver profile export/distribution workflows selected by users.
  • Activate and validate purchased premium access (currently a non-renewing 10-week pass).
  • Maintain security controls and service quality.

4. Storage and security

  • Progress/profile records are stored locally on device.
  • Sensitive local payloads are encrypted at rest with Keychain-backed key handling where available.
  • Optional biometric app lock is supported.
  • We apply reasonable safeguards against unauthorised access, loss, and misuse.

5. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal information. Data may be disclosed only where required for app operation, configured delivery services, or legal obligations.

6. Access, correction, complaints

For access/correction/complaints: capstoneready@outlook.com.

7. Updates

We may update this policy. The current version and effective date are shown on this page.
