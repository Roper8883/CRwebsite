What free access covers

  • Core app access, support, and legal/help links.
  • Profiling and readiness workflow support.
  • Learning workspace access and free quiz lanes.
  • Progress and reminder support where those features are available.

Current confirmed premium state for the active iOS public release

Current release notes in this repo confirm a non-renewing iOS premium lane positioned as Premium 10-Week Sprint, focused on General Knowledge Premium.

Broader plan lengths and additional premium lanes may exist in architecture or future store setup, but they should not be treated as live unless the current app/store build surfaces them as active.

Platform billing, refunds, and restore handling

The website does not currently sell consumer premium directly.

Where iOS premium access is offered, billing is handled in-app through Apple. Restore handling is also expected to happen in-app.

Refund handling and consumer rights should be read together with the relevant platform rules and any non-excludable consumer protections that apply.

Coming soon, staged, or temporarily unavailable features

The product family may show some premium lanes, tutorial-related features, or other release-stage items as coming soon, staged, or temporarily unavailable while content is rebuilt or while rollout is still controlled.

Those states do not create an entitlement until the relevant feature is actually released and active for your build.

Entitlement support

If you believe premium access or restore handling is wrong, first use the in-app restore or account path where available, then contact Support with your app, region, device, and build details.
