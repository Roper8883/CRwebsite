What to include in your support request

  • The name of the app you are using: CapstoneReady, AM2Ready, or JourneymanReady.
  • Your device model and operating system version.
  • Your app version/build if you can see it.
  • A short description of the issue and what you were trying to do.
  • Screenshots if they help explain the problem.

Common support topics

  • Sign in, account creation, and password reset
  • Quiz access and region-specific content issues
  • Profiling entries, attachments, and evidence workflows
  • Support for review, validation, or submission behaviour
  • Legal, privacy, or account-deletion enquiries
