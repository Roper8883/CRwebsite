What these rules cover

These rules apply where the product allows profile entries, photos, documents, certificates, notes, or other evidence to be created, attached, reviewed, exported, or routed through a workflow.

You must have the right to upload the material

You must only upload material that you are lawfully permitted to provide.

That includes respecting workplace confidentiality, privacy, client restrictions, employer policies, training-provider requirements, and any legal or contractual obligations that apply to the material.

Do not use the product as a loophole for sensitive workplace material

Do not upload confidential customer, employer, site, or training-provider material unless you are authorised to do so and the upload is appropriate for the workflow.

Do not assume that because the app allows an upload slot, every document or photo is safe or lawful to store there.

Who may see the material

Where the product supports review, employer-linked approvals, dispatch, or related workflow visibility, uploaded content may be shown to the parties involved in that workflow.

Use care before uploading material that includes third-party personal information or sensitive workplace context.

Material we may restrict or remove

  • Unlawful or infringing material
  • Material uploaded without proper rights or authority
  • Abusive, harmful, or misleading content
  • Content that is clearly outside the intended workflow and trust boundaries of the product

Institutional and enterprise-use boundary

Supporting evidence only unless formally accepted by the relevant provider or regulator and licensed by Spark's Capstone Ready.

RTO/SRTO or equivalent body use, storage, ingestion, and reliance is prohibited without prior written permission and an active commercial agreement.
