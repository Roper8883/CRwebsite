These products support preparation. They do not replace the official pathway.

CapstoneReady and the broader Sparky's Capstone Ready family are training, revision, readiness, profiling, and workplace-support tools.

They do not replace official licensing decisions, formal training-provider requirements, apprenticeship contracts, supervisor duties, site obligations, or regulatory requirements.

No guarantee of qualification, licensing, or employment

Using the apps or the public website does not guarantee a pass, qualification, licence, employment outcome, or official acceptance of any profiling evidence or workflow record.

You remain responsible for meeting the official standards, evidence requirements, and obligations that apply to your pathway and region.

Official laws, standards, and provider rules still control

Electrical laws, standards, codes, licensing rules, training-provider requirements, and workplace obligations remain controlled by the relevant official bodies, employers, and providers.

The app family helps organise preparation and workflow support around those systems. It does not replace them.

Workplace and employer obligations remain separate

Employer-linked workflow features support review and readiness visibility, but they do not transfer, reduce, or replace workplace supervision duties, safety obligations, or employer compliance responsibilities.
