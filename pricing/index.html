Billing reality

The website does not currently sell premium directly.

Where consumer premium is available, purchases are made in the app through the relevant platform billing flow. For the current iOS public lane, that means Apple billing.

Purchases

Handled in-app through Apple ID on iOS

Current iOS product notes and in-app copy both point to Apple-managed billing and restore handling.

Refunds and entitlements

Platform rules still matter

Platform-managed billing and consumer-law rights still apply. The website should not invent a direct website refund policy that conflicts with platform handling.

Support

Use the app and support lane for restore or entitlement issues

If access looks wrong, use the in-app restore path where available and contact support with your app, region, and build details.

Premium status lives beside the rest of the app controls

Billing, restores, and support should feel like part of the same trusted product flow rather than a disconnected upsell experience.

Availability note: Plan names, prices, and available premium lanes can vary by app, region, store setup, and release stage. Only treat a lane as live when the app and store surface it as active for your build.
